# BACKEND & GAMIFICATION ENGINE
- **XP Bounties:** Every play carries a mathematical weight. When a play is deployed, it assigns an XP Bounty based on total route distance and complexity.
- **Biometric Burn:** Routes inherently drain stamina and spike heart rates based on physical distance.
- **Skill Trees & Ghosts:** Players possess RPG stats (Speed, Stamina). Predictive "Success Probabilities" are calculated dynamically by comparing a Player's base stats against the assigned Route Difficulty. This data is visualized via Predictive Holograms on the frontend.
